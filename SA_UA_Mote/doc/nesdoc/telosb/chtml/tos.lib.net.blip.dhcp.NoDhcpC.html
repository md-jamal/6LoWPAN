Component: tos.lib.net.blip.dhcp.NoDhcpC

module NoDhcpC

Component for doing compile-time address allocation. Wired by the stack, sets a static address based on IN6_PREFIX and TOS_NODE_ID on boot. Useful for development or of you want to hard-code addresses.
